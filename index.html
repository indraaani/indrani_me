About

Hello, Internet!

I'm Indrani, a digital marketer from Mumbai with a penchant for all things nerdy. Polyhedral dice, tabletop RPGs, video games, gardening, reading, cooking, and alt-rock music are all very dear to my heart.

I'm incredibly passionate about interactive and narrative-driven tabletop and video games and the impact they can have on people. I've poured this love into co-founding Desis & Dragons, India's largest TTRPG community. I'm also the founder and owner of Nonagon Dice, India's first and only artisan handmade dice brand that has received international recognition and praise.

I began my professional marketing career at 17, working with various American tech companies to build their brands and connect with their diverse consumer bases. I graduated from Sophia College for Women with a Bachelor's Degree in Advertising & Mass Media with a GPA of 3.7 out of 4.

Find my CV here, and if you'd like to reach me, head to my contact page.

Personal Projects

Nonagon Dice

Nonagon Dice spawned out of my love for collecting RPG dice. One day, I went from "I'm going to buy more dice!" to "I'm going to make more dice!". At Nonagon Dice, I create artisan dice for table-top roleplaying games. Since its inception in 2020, Nonagon Dice has received international recognition within the TTRPG space.

Desis & Dragons

I am one of the three co-founders of Desis & Dragons, a TTRPG content channel and community in India. What started as a quest to bring the joy of tabletop RPGs to people across India has led to the creation of India's largest TTRPG community. As a kid, I wished for a minority, neurodivergent, and LGBT-friendly community of nerds. At 21, I'm making my wish come true.

The Gamesage

Though the Indian gaming community is growing, there is a lack of discourse around video games. My friends and I set out to change that with The Gamesage. At The Gamesage, we experience video games not just from a consumerist perspective, but also from a critical academic lens.
