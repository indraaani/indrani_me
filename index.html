About

I'm Indrani, a digital marketer and entreprenuer with 4 years of experience. I have worked with various American companies to build their brands and grow their diverse consumer bases. I am the founder and owner of Nonagon Dice, India's first and only artisan handmade dice brand that has received international recognition and praise. I am also the co-founder of Desis & Dragons, India's largest TTRPG community and content channel.

I'm incredibly passionate about interactive storytelling mediums such as video games and the impact they can have on people. I'm currently looking for marketing positions within the gaming industry.

When I'm not working, I spend time crafting polyhedral dice, playing tabletop RPGs and video games, gardening, cooking, or fishing for Steam achievements.

Projects

Desis & Dragons

I am one of the three co-founders of Desis & Dragons, a TTRPG content channel and community in India. What started as a quest to bring the joy of tabletop RPGs to people across India has led to the creation of India's largest TTRPG community. Our work has received international recognition by prominent figures in the TTRPG space. We foster a space that is welcoming to minorities, neurodivergent people, and the LGBT+ community, shaping the future of what the TTRPG landscape in India will look like.

Nonagon Dice

Nonagon Dice India's first and largest artisan handmade dice business. My work has been featured in a national news outlet and has received international recognition within the TTRPG space. Since its inception in 2021, Nonagon Dice has garnered a world-wide audience of over 5,000 people and counting.

The Gamesage

At The Gamesage, we're redefining what it means to be an Indian gamer. Our small team is creating video content and podcasts that discuss video games across all genres, from AAA titles to indie gems. We're carving a niche in the game-journalism and game-entertainment space, bringing a brand new perspective to Indian video game consumers.
